About CleanPro

Our Mission to Make India's Bathrooms Sparkle

Our Story

Founded in 2024, CleanPro began with a simple observation: most toilet cleaners in the market were either too expensive, ineffective, or contained harmful chemicals.

Our founder, Mr. Rajesh Kumar, noticed this while running a small cleaning service in Delhi. He saw families struggling with hard water stains, foul odors, and unhygienic bathrooms despite using multiple products.

That's when we decided to create something better - a toilet cleaner that's affordable, effective, and safe for every Indian household.

Starting from a small garage in West Delhi, we've now served 10,000+ happy customers across North India.

Our Mission

To provide every Indian household with affordable, effective, and safe toilet cleaning solutions that actually work.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted toilet care brand, recognized for quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Our Values

Honesty, Quality, Innovation, and Customer First - these values guide everything we do.
